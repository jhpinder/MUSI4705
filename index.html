3D Modeling/Printing:

The 3D modeling for the entire project was done in SketchUp. Since it doesn't have a native .stl export function, an extension that allowed SketchUp to export .stl files was downloaded and installed.

To begin the process of modeling the required pieces, we first needed to create a model of the wood box and divider grid. This allowed us to design the pieces around the actual dimensions of the box before the entire box was complete
